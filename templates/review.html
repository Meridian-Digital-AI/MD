{% if campaign.google_ads %}
1

Google Ads — Add payment

  1. Go to ads.google.com and sign in with your email
  2. If prompted, accept the invitation to manage your ad account
  3. Click the Tools icon (spanner) in the top menu
  4. Under "Billing", click Billing settings
  5. Click Add payment method and enter your card details
  6. Once payment is added, go to Campaigns and click Enable on your campaign

Your daily budget is set to £{{ campaign.google_ads.daily_budget_gbp }}/day (~£{{ '%.0f'|format(campaign.google_ads.daily_budget_gbp * 30) }}/month). You can adjust this anytime in Google Ads.

Meta Ads — Add payment

  2. Navigate to Business Settings → Payment methods

  4. Then go to Ads Manager (adsmanager.facebook.com)
  5. Find your campaign and toggle it from Off to On
