Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{ last_updated }}

This Privacy Policy describes how Meridian Digital ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects personal data when you use our website and ad-management service (the "Service"). We are based in the United Kingdom and we comply with the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who we are

Meridian Digital is the data controller for the personal data described in this policy. If you have any questions about this policy or your data, contact us at hello@meridiandigital.co.uk.

2. What data we collect

When you use the Service, we collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Account data: your email address and a session token used to keep you signed in.
  • Business information you provide: business name, industry, location, service area, monthly ad budget, marketing goal, unique selling points, target audience description, and (optionally) website URL, phone number, and additional context.
  • Ad campaigns generated for you: AI-generated Google Ads and Meta Ads campaign drafts based on the business information you submit.
  • Billing data: we use Stripe to process payments. We do not store your full card details on our servers — Stripe handles that. We do store a Stripe customer ID and your subscription plan.
  • Technical data: basic server logs (IP address, request paths, timestamps) used for debugging and abuse prevention.

3. How we use your data

We use your personal data to:

  • Provide the Service — generate ad campaigns, push them to Google Ads and Meta Ads on your behalf, and let you review and approve them.
  • Take payment via Stripe and manage your subscription.
  • Send you transactional emails (welcome, "your campaign is ready", "campaign approved").
  • Comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests.
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents.

4. Legal basis for processing

We rely on the following legal bases under UK GDPR:

  • Performance of a contract — to deliver the Service you signed up for.
  • Legitimate interests — to keep the Service secure, prevent abuse, and improve it.
  • Legal obligation — to keep accounting and tax records.
  • Consent — where required, e.g. for non-essential cookies.

5. Sharing your data

We share data only with the third-party processors needed to run the Service:

  • Stripe — payment processing.
  • Anthropic — generates the campaign drafts from your business information using the Claude API.
  • Google (Google Ads API) — to push approved campaigns to your Google Ads account.
  • Meta (Marketing API) — to push approved campaigns to your Meta Ads account.
  • Resend — to send transactional emails.
  • Our hosting provider — to operate the servers that run the Service.

We do not sell your personal data and we do not share it with third parties for their own marketing.

6. International transfers

Some of the processors above are based outside the UK (for example in the EU or US). Where data is transferred internationally, we rely on the safeguards required by UK GDPR (such as the UK International Data Transfer Agreement or adequacy decisions).

7. How long we keep your data

We keep account data and campaign drafts for as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period afterwards to handle support requests, disputes, and legal obligations. Billing records are kept for at least six years to comply with UK tax law. You can ask us to delete your account at any time.

8. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restrict or object to our processing.
  • Receive your data in a portable format.
  • Withdraw consent where we rely on it.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@meridiandigital.co.uk.

9. Cookies

We set a single first-party session cookie (session_token) so we can keep you signed in. This cookie is strictly necessary for the Service to function and does not require consent under PECR. We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies on our own site.

10. Security

We protect your data using HTTPS in transit, access controls, and standard security practices. No internet service can be guaranteed 100% secure, but we take the protection of your data seriously and notify users and the ICO of any breach as required by law.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent change. Material changes will be communicated to you by email or via a notice in the Service.

12. Contact

Questions about this policy or how we handle your data? Email hello@meridiandigital.co.uk.
